Why SwasthAI Matters

The Problem

Lack of Access

In many parts of India, immediate access to medical advice is a challenge, leading to delayed care and self-medication risks.

Our Mission

Empowerment

To provide a safe, reliable, and accessible first point of contact for healthcare queries, empowering users to make informed decisions.

The Solution

AI as a First Doctor

SwasthAI acts as a virtual doctor, offering instant guidance for non-emergency situations and flagging potential risks in prescriptions.

The Impact

Safer Healthcare

By promoting awareness and providing data-driven suggestions, we aim to reduce medication errors and encourage timely professional consultation.

About SwasthAI

SwasthAI ("Health AI") is an innovative project designed to bridge the gap in primary healthcare access. Leveraging the power of large language models and retrieval-augmented generation, we offer a tool that is not just a symptom checker, but a comprehensive safety net for initial medical queries.

Our goal is to make healthcare guidance more accessible and safer for everyone, from bustling cities to remote villages.
